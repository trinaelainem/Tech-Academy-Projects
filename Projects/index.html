TOP 10 CUTEST DOG BREEDS

GOLDEN RETRIEVER

Goldens are outgoing, trustworthy, and eager-to-please family dogs, and relatively easy to train.

SHIH TZU

Shih Tzus are incredibly affectionate and loving, loyal, and eager to please their loved ones.

DACHSHUND

Dachshunds are smart and playful, and can be quite stubborn.

JAPANESE SPITZ

The Japanese Spitz are known for their great courage, affection, devotion and amiability.

SHIBA INU

Shiba Inus are alert, loyal and lively, and they tend to form strong bonds with their owners.

DALMATIAN

Dalmatians are highly energetic, playful, and sensitive dogs.

COCKER SPANIEL

Cocker Spaniels are kind and gentle making them excellent dogs for first-time owners.

SIBERIAN HUSKY

The Siberian Husky is active, mischievous, and playful, and is outgoing towards strangers.

CHIHUAHUA

Chihuahuas have big personalities and range from feisty and outgoing to shy and timid.
